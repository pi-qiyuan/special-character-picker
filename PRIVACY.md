# Privacy Policy

This extension (Special Character Picker) is designed with privacy as a top priority.

1. **Data Collection**: We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data or chat history.
2. **Data Processing**: All chat content processing and file generation are performed locally in your browser.
3. **Third-party Access**: No data is ever shared with third parties or external servers.
4. **Permissions**: The extension only requires access to the specific AI chat websites to identify and export your conversations upon your request.

Your data never leaves your machine.
