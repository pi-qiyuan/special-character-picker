# Special Character Picker: Symbols, Emoji & Math

Stop breaking your creative flow to search for symbols! Special Character Picker is a lightweight productivity tool designed to keep your hands "close to your heart." Whether you are writing code, academic papers, or social media posts, get the exact character you need in a split second.

Designed for writers, developers, and students who value a seamless "deep work" experience.

### 🌟 THREE POWERFUL MODES
-----------------------------------
- **Direct Insert**: Instantly inject the chosen character directly into the active text field on your current webpage.

- **Smart Copy**: Copy any character to your clipboard with a single click, ready to paste into any desktop app (Word, Slack, etc.).

- **Edit Mode**: A mini-workspace to assemble multiple characters and text before copying or inserting.

### 🚀 KEY FEATURES
-----------------------------------
- ✅ **Categorized Library**: Effortlessly browse through Math symbols, Arrows, Currency, Punctuation, and Emojis.
- ✅ **Smart Memory**: The extension remembers your last category and mode, so it’s always exactly where you left it.
- ✅ **Recents & Favorites**: Access your most frequently used symbols in the "Recent" bar—no more digging.
- ✅ **Non-Intrusive Design**: A minimalist UI that opens quickly and stays out of your way.

### 📖 HOW IT WORKS
-----------------------------------
Open the extension via the toolbar or a shortcut.
Find your symbol (via categories or your favorites).
Click to Copy or Insert—your flow remains uninterrupted!
You may add symbols to your favorite by right click on the symbol.
